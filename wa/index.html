"My Journey of Academic Excellence & Multilingual Proficiency"

In the dynamic landscape of the 90s, my educational voyage commenced, shaping the foundation of my present expertise. Achieving my Bacalaureate in Foreign Languages in 2008 marked the initial stride in my pursuit of knowledge. Subsequently, I attained a Bachelor's Degree in General English in 2012, delving deeper into the realm of languages. Advancing further, I embraced the rich domain of Literature and Civilization, earning a Master's Degree, and currently envisioning the pursuit of a Doctorate. My linguistic proficiency spans a C2 level in French, complemented by a solid grasp of the Spanish language, augmented by diverse foreign training experiences. My journey epitomizes a commitment to continuous learning, scholarly endeavors, and multilingual proficiency.

"Teaching Journey & Literary Passion: Exploring My Expertise"

Since 2012, my passion for teaching has led me through diverse educational arenas, encompassing primary, middle, and secondary levels across various private institutions. Presently, I am engaged in imparting knowledge and shaping minds at the university level, embracing the role with enthusiasm and dedication. Beyond the classroom, I am fueled by a fervent love for the written word. My creative pursuits extend to crafting poems, weaving captivating stories, and even delving into the intricate world of novels. Additionally, I've contributed my expertise in educational spheres by creating enriching school books. These endeavors stand as a testament to my commitment to education and the art of storytelling, blending teaching expertise with a fervent passion for the written narrative.
